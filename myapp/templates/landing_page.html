Why Choose Us

Choose Tras For a better experience with your child

Down syndrome, also known as trisomy 21, is a genetic condition caused by the presence of an extra copy of chromosome 21. Despite challenges, individuals with Down syndrome can lead fulfilling lives with the right support and opportunities for growth.

Let's Try A Simple Activity!

Parents can engage their child with Down syndrome in age-appropriate games and activities designed to help them recognize emotions and improve their communication skills. TRAS provides interactive experiences to encourage children to identify and express their feelings in a fun and supportive environment.

"Play is key for every child, including those with Down syndrome, fostering joy, learning, and connection."
